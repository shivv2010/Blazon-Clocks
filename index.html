Horological Excellence

The Blazon Legacy

Founded in the Swiss Jura mountains, Blazon has spent seven decades perfecting the art of haute horlogerie. Each timepiece is a testament to the uncompromising standards set by our founder, Henri Blazon.

Our atelier brings together master watchmakers, engineers, and artisans who share a singular passion for mechanical perfection. Every component, from the smallest screw to the most complex tourbillon, is crafted with obsessive attention to detail.

Blazon watches are more than instruments of time - they are heirlooms that embody the pinnacle of human craftsmanship.

The Blazon Standard

Exceptional Materials

We source only the finest 18k gold, platinum, and rare alloys, paired with flawless diamonds and precious stones hand-selected by our gemologists.

In-House Movements

Every Blazon caliber is designed, manufactured, assembled, and decorated entirely within our Swiss manufacture, meeting the highest chronometric standards.

Master Certification

Each completed watch undergoes 500 hours of rigorous testing before receiving the Blazon Master Certification - our personal guarantee of excellence.
